We are a family run flower & vegetable farm. We sell speciality flower & vegetable seeds, gardening tools, & books.

Set in North Carolina's Blue Ridge Mountains, our fields reflect the colors of the season. Research is at the heart of our operation, and we conduct extensive trials to find the highest quality of flower and vegetable seeds.

A few years into production, we began cultivating a farm shop. The store quickly was filled with speciality flower and vegetable seeds. As demand picked up, we expanded our products to provide gardening tools and gifts. Below is a preview selection of our garden products.
